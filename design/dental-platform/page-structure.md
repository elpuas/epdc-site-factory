# Dental Platform Page Structure

## Canonical Sitemap

The canonical dental sitemap for Costa Rica should begin with:

- Home
- About
- Services
- Individual Service Pages
- Doctors or Team
- Testimonials
- Blog
- FAQ
- Contact
- Privacy Policy

Optional future expansion can include:

- Locations
- New Patients
- Financing
- Individual Doctor Pages
- Category landing pages for specialties

## Home

Purpose:
Introduce the clinic, build trust quickly, and move visitors toward appointment action.

Primary conversion goal:
Drive appointment requests through WhatsApp, phone, or appointment CTA.

Required data:

- clinic identity
- hero message
- primary and secondary CTA labels
- featured services
- clinic intro
- differentiators
- doctor highlights
- review highlights
- location snapshot
- blog highlights

SEO intent:
Target broad local dental intent such as clinic name, dentist in target city, family dentistry, and commercial brand queries.

Recommended sections:

1. Sticky header
2. Dental hero
3. Main service cards
4. Clinic intro
5. Benefits or differentiators
6. Dental specialties preview
7. Doctor highlights
8. Testimonials or Google reviews
9. Location snapshot with hours
10. Strong appointment CTA
11. Blog preview
12. Footer

## About

Purpose:
Explain the clinic story, care philosophy, environment, and professional credibility.

Primary conversion goal:
Increase trust and reduce hesitation before contacting the clinic.

Required data:

- clinic story
- mission or care philosophy
- certifications or memberships
- office environment notes
- doctor summaries
- trust content

SEO intent:
Support branded queries, clinic-name searches, and trust-oriented local research behavior.

Recommended sections:

1. Intro hero or page heading
2. Clinic story
3. Care philosophy
4. Team preview
5. Technology or quality standards
6. Trust logos or certifications
7. CTA to book an appointment

## Services

Purpose:
Provide an overview of the clinic's treatment offering and route users into deeper service pages.

Primary conversion goal:
Help visitors identify the right treatment path and continue into consultation intent.

Required data:

- service categories
- service summaries
- supporting icons or imagery references
- CTA labels

SEO intent:
Capture broad service-intent searches and distribute authority to individual treatment pages.

Recommended sections:

1. Services intro
2. Service card grid
3. Specialty grouping or categories
4. Trust or reassurance block
5. CTA to schedule or ask on WhatsApp

## Individual Service Pages

Purpose:
Capture treatment-intent traffic and convert higher-intent visitors.

Primary conversion goal:
Drive treatment-specific consultation requests.

Required data:

- service name
- summary
- patient-fit guidance
- benefits
- treatment process
- FAQs
- CTA labels
- related services
- review or trust support

SEO intent:
Target treatment-specific local searches such as whitening, implants, orthodontics, veneers, endodontics, or emergency dental care.

Recommended sections:

1. Service hero
2. Treatment overview
3. Who it is for
4. Benefits
5. Process or what to expect
6. Trust block or doctor relevance
7. FAQs
8. Appointment CTA
9. Related services or blog links

## Doctors Or Team

Purpose:
Present the professionals behind the clinic and strengthen credibility.

Primary conversion goal:
Improve trust and encourage appointment action from patients who choose based on provider confidence.

Required data:

- doctor names
- titles
- biographies
- specialties
- credentials
- optional photo references
- trust associations

SEO intent:
Support brand trust, doctor-name searches, and credibility for medical content.

Recommended sections:

1. Team intro
2. Doctor grid
3. Expanded bios
4. Certifications or associations
5. CTA to schedule with the clinic

## Testimonials

Purpose:
Aggregate patient trust signals in one destination.

Primary conversion goal:
Reduce friction for skeptical or comparison-stage visitors.

Required data:

- testimonial excerpts
- attribution rules
- review source notes when approved
- optional rating summaries

SEO intent:
Support trust-oriented branded searches and internal linking from service and home pages.

Recommended sections:

1. Page intro
2. Review highlights
3. Testimonial grid or list
4. Trust CTA
5. Contact CTA

## Blog

Purpose:
Support local SEO, treatment education, and internal linking.

Primary conversion goal:
Move informational visitors toward service or contact pages.

Required data:

- article list
- article metadata
- categories or tags
- featured image references
- related service mapping

SEO intent:
Target informational and early-stage treatment queries while reinforcing local relevance.

Recommended sections:

1. Blog archive heading
2. Featured article
3. Article grid
4. Category filters in the future if needed
5. CTA into services or appointments

## FAQ

Purpose:
Answer common objections and reduce pre-contact uncertainty.

Primary conversion goal:
Help users self-qualify and move into appointment action.

Required data:

- FAQ items
- category or page associations
- contact CTA labels

SEO intent:
Support FAQ rich-result opportunities where appropriate and reinforce service-specific relevance.

Recommended sections:

1. FAQ intro
2. General clinic FAQs
3. Treatment FAQs
4. Payment and insurance FAQs
5. CTA to ask by WhatsApp or book

## Contact

Purpose:
Provide all direct contact and visit information.

Primary conversion goal:
Convert ready-to-act visitors by removing logistical barriers.

Required data:

- phone
- WhatsApp
- email
- form destination
- address
- hours
- map data
- parking or arrival notes
- branch data if relevant

SEO intent:
Support local trust, consistency of business details, and location relevance.

Recommended sections:

1. Contact intro
2. Appointment form
3. Phone and WhatsApp actions
4. Address and map
5. Hours
6. Parking or arrival guidance
7. Branch details if applicable

## Privacy Policy

Purpose:
Provide legal clarity and support trust for form submissions and analytics.

Primary conversion goal:
Indirect support only; this page mainly reduces compliance risk.

Required data:

- legal entity or clinic name
- contact email
- data-handling explanation
- analytics disclosure
- form-data handling notes

SEO intent:
Usually low SEO priority, but important for trust and site completeness.

Recommended sections:

1. Scope of policy
2. Data collected
3. Contact and form handling
4. Analytics and cookies if relevant
5. Rights and contact method

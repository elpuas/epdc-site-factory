# Example Website Specification

This file is an example specification only. It exists to demonstrate the kind of project brief that future EPDC Site Factory workflows may consume.

## Client Name

BrightSmile Dental Clinic

## Industry

Dental care and local healthcare services

## Business Goals

- Increase appointment requests from local search traffic
- Present a trustworthy and modern clinic brand
- Clearly explain core services for new patients
- Make it easy for visitors to contact the clinic
- Support future growth with reusable content sections

## Technical Requirements

- Website generated with Astro
- Prepared for future Supabase-backed contact or appointment workflows
- Mobile-friendly layout
- Fast page load performance
- Clear content structure for local SEO
- Simple deployment-ready project structure

## Content Requirements

- Home page with hero section and core value proposition
- About section introducing the clinic and team
- Services section covering general dentistry, cosmetic dentistry, and teeth cleaning
- Testimonials section with placeholder patient quotes
- Contact section with phone, address, hours, and inquiry form placeholder
- FAQ section for common patient questions

## Acceptance Criteria

- The website communicates professionalism and trust
- The main services are easy to understand
- Contact actions are clearly visible on mobile and desktop
- The structure supports future SEO improvements
- The generated project is organized for future extension
- The implementation aligns with the project specification
